NYU GALA OFFICIAL TRAILER

NYU GALA is an annual performance hold by NYU Chinese Students and Scholars Association. It is tedious to see a trailer as a random mix of dancing videos. As the director of the official trailer, I come up with a simple storyline. The trailer has a beginning (dancers and actors walked into their separate rehearsal studios), middle (they start to perform together), end (the climax of each shows). I also design a black screen just right before the climax, to let the audience get ready for the show!
